Adventure Works 2019 Project

Introduction

The dashboard provides targeted insights for Adventure Works 2019, specifically regarding sales information. It presents data in an easy-to-understand manner, allowing the company to gain a deeper understanding of its data.

  • Technology: Power Bi - SQL
  • Completed: 20/8/2024
  • Target: Presenting the data in a simple way to help the company make the right decisions to develop the company and so that the company realizes its strengths and weaknesses.

Data Preparation
I gathered the important columns that I want to use in SQL with these codes and then transferred them to Power Query to complete the process by splitting them into smaller tables and creating relationships between the tables.




Detailed Insights

    The dashboard provides a multitude of insights, derived from in-depth analysis of the data:

  • Revenues Analysis : It shows Revenues by Region, Year, Month, Quarter, and the number of orders.
  • Categories Analysis : It shows Revenues by Categories, Online Orders and Customers by Region.
  • Prouducts Analysis : It shows Tax Amounts and Freights by Products, Top-Grossing Products, Best-Selling Products and Actual Sales Vs Quota by Sales Person.
  • Discounts Analysis : It shows Sales Current Year Vs Last Year by Sales Person and Revenues by Discount.
